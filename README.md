# 🚀 Decoding Customer Churn: Data-Driven Insights for Retention

> Turning raw customer data into actionable business decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
Customer churn is a critical challenge for subscription-based businesses. In this project, I conducted **data-driven exploratory data analysis (EDA)** to identify key factors influencing customer churn and translate findings into **actionable retention strategies**.

The focus was not just on visualization, but on **deriving business-relevant insights** that can support decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Quantify churn and assess its business impact
- Identify key drivers influencing customer churn
- Analyze behavioral and demographic patterns
- Generate actionable insights for retention improvement

---

## 📂 Dataset
- **Domain**: Telecom / Subscription-based service
- **Features Include**:
  - Customer demographics (Gender, Senior Citizen)
  - Account details (Tenure, Contract Type)
  - Services subscribed
  - Payment methods
  - **Churn (Target Variable)**

## 🔄 Workflow

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- 📉 **~26% customers churn**, indicating a significant retention challenge
- ⚡ **Early-stage customers show higher churn** → need stronger onboarding strategies
- 📆 **Month-to-month contracts drive maximum churn** → low commitment risk
- 👴 **Senior citizens exhibit higher churn rates** → potential usability/pricing concerns
- 🔗 **Higher service adoption reduces churn** → strong product engagement
- 💳 **Electronic check users churn more** → possible payment friction

---

## 💡 Business Impact
The analysis reveals that churn is **behavior-driven, not random**.

👉 Key strategic recommendations:
- Improve onboarding experience for new users
- Promote long-term contract adoption
- Encourage multi-service usage for higher engagement
- Optimize payment methods to reduce friction
